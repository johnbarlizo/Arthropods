Cash On Delivery

Thank you for placing your order using the Cash On Delivery option. Please follow the steps below for the next process:

  1. Step 1: Order Confirmation

    2. Once you submit your order, you will receive an email confirming your order details.

  2. Step 2: Order Processing

    3. Our team will review and process your order. This may take some time, so please be patient.

  3. Step 3: Order Shipment

    4. Once your order is ready to be shipped, we will send you a notification email with the tracking information.

    5. Our courier partner will deliver the package to your provided address. Please ensure someone is available to receive the order.

  5. Step 5: Payment

    6. Upon delivery, you can pay the total amount in cash to the delivery person. Please have the exact amount ready.

If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to contact our customer service.
